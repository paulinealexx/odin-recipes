Brownies

Oozing chocolate brownies are the way to go! Check out this simple brownie recipe for a mouth watering chocolatey treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups sugar (250 g)
  • 12 tablespoons melted butter
  • 8 oz of semi-sweet chocolate (225 g)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder (30 g)
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour (95 g)
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit (or 180 degrees Celsius), and line an 8-inch square baking dish with parchment paper.
  2. Chop your chocolate into chunks and melt half of it in the microwave in 20-second intervals. Save the second half for later.
  3. In a large bowl, mix the butter and sugar, then beat in the eggs and vanilla for 1-2 minutes until the mixture becomes fluffy and light in colour.
  4. Whisk in the melted chocolate (not too hot) and sift in the flour, cocoa powder, and salt. Add in the dry ingredients, being careful not to overmix to avoid a cake-like texture.
  5. Add in the chocolate chunks and transfer the batter to the baking dish.
  6. Bake for 20-25 minutes, depending on how fudgy you like rownies, then cool completely.
  7. That's it! Slice and serve the brownies.
